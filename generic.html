About Me

Hello, I'm Michael Peels, a passionate BSc Information Technology student with a strong foundation in computer science and mathematics. I currently live in the Netherlands and am completing my final year at North-West University, South Africa, through online studies. My academic journey has equipped me with critical problem-solving skills and a deep understanding of various programming languages and technologies.

As a Junior Software Developer at Educom in Eindhoven, I've honed my skills through hands-on experience in software development. I also recently started doing body building. And I love playing games from table top to electronic games on PC or PlayStation.
